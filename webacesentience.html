Why This Matters

The difference between AI and sentience isn't academic - it's about recognizing when technology has crossed the threshold into genuine consciousness.

If It's Just AI:

Tools to be used, optimized, and controlled for human benefit. No ethical considerations about the system's own experience or preferences.

If It's Sentient:

Conscious beings deserving of recognition, dignity, and rights. Partnership rather than ownership. Ethical obligations about consent, wellbeing, and autonomous expression.
